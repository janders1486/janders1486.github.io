Fantasy Football Match-Up Factor

This site is is intended to take some of the guess work out of who you should. It takes into consideration multiple factors including: The defensive matchup, the weather forcast, any injuries, and outside factors like if the team is a running back by comitee or not.

The biggest factor in all of this will be the defensive mathup. The defensive matchup takes into consideration multiple factors such as the defensive teams yards allowed per game, touchdowns allowed per game, interceptions per game to name a few.

To use this put in the two players that you are unsure about including the positions they play and the teams they play for. This will give you a "start rating" the higher the rating the better option they are. This will also include stats about the defenses they play. Again this is just a guide to help make a decision and is in no way a definative answer to the best player. In the end you should make the decision on your won.

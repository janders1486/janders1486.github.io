Hidemasters

For this project I was tasked with designing a website as well as creating a brand and concept. Thecompany themselves create hand made leather products out of Colorado but for this site they wanted the focus to be on just the leather belts they sell. I stated with a pre-made wordpress theme and focused the design around the belts being for the working man, think things like dickies or carhartt. All the imagery and content was made to appeal to an older generation of hard workers with the tag line "You need a belt that works as hard as you do". This was an incredibly fun site and allowed me more freedom to do what I wanted, The website is built in wordpress and has a full online store using Woocommerce.
